    Two very useful references are available in the library of Centrale Lille. One of them (Hastie & Tibshirani 2009) is even available in pdf here.

    • Pattern Recognition & Machine Learning, C. Bishop, Springer, 2006.
    • The Elements of Statistcial Learning, T. Hastie & R. Tibshirani & J. Friedman, Springer 2009
    • Machine Learning: a probabilistic perspective. Kevin P. Murphy, The MIT Press, 2012.
